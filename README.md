## Can be installed as a phone app – Important Note
**Look for the blue button inside the page that says:**  
*"انقر لتحميل الصفحة كتطبيق هاتف"* (Click to install as phone app)

- This button appears automatically if your browser supports PWA installation
- Click it to install the page as a **native app** on your phone
- After installation, you'll have an app icon on your home screen
- The app works **offline** and opens faster
  
> ⚠️ If you don't see the button, your browser may not support PWA, or the app is already installed.
